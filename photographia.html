PHOTOGRAPHIA

“Photography is the story we fail to put into words”. To all the shutterbugs out there, here is a chance to put your skills to good use. Photographia is the photography competition of IdEEEas 2019, where you compete with other photographers to capture our judges’ imagination. The topic for the competition will be revealed on the day of the event. Happy Snapping!

Rules:

  • Only photos taken from a mobile phone will be accepted.
  • Photos will be accepted only from the phones registered during the registrations.
  • Only soft copies will be accepted.
  • Participants will have to take the photos on the same day within allotted time.
  • Photographs should be unedited.
  • Coordinator decision will be final & binding.
  • Entry fee: Rs.50/- per participant.

    Selection Criteria:

    First Round:

  • In this round, all the participants will have to go around the campus and take two photographs according to the given theme and submit the same.

    • Second Round:

  • 10 participants will be shortlisted and they have to explain about their photographs to the judges. The shortlisted candidates will be asked to post their photos to their respective Instagram pages, tagging IEEESITSB in the photo, at the beginning of the second round. The participant that secures the maximum number of likes for their post within the time of the second round will receive a special gift. This prize is separate from the prize for the overall event.
