WIE CODE 2.0

WIE Code 2.0 is a hackathon aims to encourage and inspire more women to pursue tech.
Organizers promise a weekend filled with learning, exploring new topics, and building new friendships and amazing projects.

  • No Registration Fee included.
  • Team Leader's will hear from IEEE WIE SIT SB if there team gets selected.
  • Disobeing of any Rules Specified above will lead to disqualification of Team.
